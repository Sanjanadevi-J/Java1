Deque:
     This interface is known as double ended queue.
     It supports insertion and removal at both ends.
Methods:
      add,offer,remove,poll,peek,element.

ArrayDeque:
         This is a deque with resizeable array.
         adding and removing of elements done on both the sides.
         Null element is not stored .
         No defined capacity, it is faster than linked list and stack.
